FAQ

What Is IEEE Annual Membership Price?

IEEE Membership Fees Are 10 JOD, And IEEE WIE Membership Is Free For IEEE Members.

How Can I Join?

You Can Join By Filling Out The Membership Form, And We Will Contact You To Complete The Rest Of The Joining Procedures.

Who Can Join IEEE WIE UJ?

Membership In IEEE WIE JU Is Open To All Students At The University Of Jordan -Regardless Of Their Major - Who Want To Participate In Our Activities And Are Willing To Contribute Their Time And Effort.

What Benefits Do I Get As A Member?

As A Member Of IEEE WIE JU, You Will Gain Opportunities To Attend Workshops, Network With Students And Professionals From Various Specialties, Receive Certificates For Your Participation, And Take Part In Exciting Events. Membership Also Helps You Shape And Develop Valuable Skills In Areas Such As Marketing, Logistics, Public Relations, And More.

Should I Make An IEEE Membership When I Join IEEE WIE UJ?

Yes. IEEE WIE Membership Requires An Active IEEE Membership, Which Allows You To Access All IEEE Benefits And Participate Fully In WIE Activities.
